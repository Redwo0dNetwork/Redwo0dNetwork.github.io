Our Story

Redwood Network was founded by a group of passionate gamers who believed that online gaming should be free, accessible, and community-driven. After experiencing various challenges with traditional game servers, we decided to take matters into our own hands and build something better. Our goal is simple: create a network of reliable and fun servers for players of all types to connect and enjoy their favorite games.


We began with a few game servers, but as the community grew, so did our ambitions. Today, we offer servers for a variety of games like Minecraft, Project Zomboid, Red Dead Redemption 2, and many others. We believe in providing a space for gamers to form lasting friendships, collaborate, and compete in a positive and inclusive environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide top-tier game servers that are fast, secure, and cost-free. We aim to empower gamers by removing the financial barriers that often come with running private game servers. Through our work, we strive to enhance the gaming experience, promote community involvement, and ensure that players of all backgrounds feel welcomed and valued.

Our Values

  • Community-Centered: We believe in fostering a community where players help each other and create lasting connections.
  • Transparency: We operate with full transparency and actively listen to the needs of our community.
  • Free and Fair Gaming: We are committed to providing a gaming experience free from financial barriers and pay-to-win models.
  • Innovation: We constantly seek new ways to improve our servers, features, and community tools to keep the gaming experience fresh.

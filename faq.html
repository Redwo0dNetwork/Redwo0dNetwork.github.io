Frequently Asked Questions

What is Redwood Network?

Redwood Network is a community-driven platform providing free-to-play game servers for popular titles like Minecraft, Project Zomboid, Red Dead Redemption 2, and more. We focus on delivering seamless multiplayer experiences while removing financial barriers to entry for players.

How do I join a server?

To join one of our game servers, simply visit the server list page on our website, select the game you're interested in, and follow the instructions for joining. We strive to make the process as simple as possible, with no additional fees or complex steps.

Are the servers really free?

Yes! All of our servers are completely free to play. We believe that gaming should be accessible to everyone, which is why we cover the cost of running the servers. You wonâ€™t have to worry about paying for premium access or dealing with pay-to-win models.

Can I create my own server on Redwood Network?

Currently, we manage and maintain all of the game servers on Redwood Network. However, we are always open to community feedback and may explore the possibility of allowing users to create their own servers in the future.

What games are supported?

We currently support games such as Minecraft, Project Zomboid, and Red Dead Redemption 2. We are constantly expanding our list of supported games based on community demand and server availability. Check out the server list for the latest updates!

How do I contact support?

If you need assistance or have any questions, you can reach out to us through our contact form on the website, or you can join our Discord community for real-time help and support. We're here to help!
